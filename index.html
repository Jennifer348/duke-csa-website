Who we are

More than a student org

Duke CSA is the hub for Chinese students across all schools and programs at Duke University. We bring together undergrads, grad students, and professional students through cultural events, career programming, and a tight-knit community.

Whether you're navigating campus life, looking for internship advice, or just want to celebrate Lunar New Year with people who get it — CSA is where you belong.
